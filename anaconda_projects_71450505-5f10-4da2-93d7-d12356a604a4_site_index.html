Inventory Reorder Planner

Turns an orders CSV into safety stock, reorder points, and MOQ-aware recommendations. Built in Python (pandas + numpy + openpyxl).

What it does

  • Clean orders → daily demand per SKU (28-day mean & std)
  • Safety = z × std × √L; ROP = mean × L + safety
  • Target = mean × (L+H) + safety; MOQ/case-pack aware
  • Excel export with tabs: Reorder, DailyDemand, CleanOrders, ConfigUsed
